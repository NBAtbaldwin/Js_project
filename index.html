Cooking up a beat...

Welcome to tinyDAW! Never used a digital audio workstation before? No problem. Check out these instructions or just hit

a bunch of times to generate random tunes.

  • Tap keys '1' through '=', 'q' through ']', and 'a' through 'enter' to play sounds
  • Click this button to record your keystrokes
  • Click this button to hear a metronome while playing or recording is enabled
  • Drag the slider to change tempo while playing or paused
  • Click on a grid square or drag across the grid to create notes
  • Click buttons that look like this to select instruments
  • Click 'clear' buttons to remove all notes from the corresponding grid
